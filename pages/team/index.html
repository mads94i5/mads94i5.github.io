OVERVIEW

I am a solo developer and I began learning Unity/C# and started development in October 2018 as a spare time hobby. In August 2022, I registered the company in anticipation of getting on Steam.

The name of the company has a dual meaning. Firstly, 'swalk' is the gaming nickname I have gone by since the age of 13. Secondly, it is also something that used to be written on the back of letters in the format: S.W.A.L.K. and it means; sealed with a loving kiss. This goes hand-in-hand with the fact that I am creating a work of passion.

I would love to expand the company and work with others, but it requires some form of financial success as this is a near zero-budget venture. My business model is; "don't spend something you don't have".
