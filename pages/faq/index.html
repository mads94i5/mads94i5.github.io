FAQ

WHY ISN'T LEGENDS OF MYTHOLOGY ON X AND Y PLATFORMS?

I would love to bring the game to all platforms, but to start off with I am targetting Steam. The game has no budget behind it and thus, I have to do one at a time.


HOW LONG IS THE DEMO?

First time playtests by players have run between 20 minutes to over 1 hour.


WILL THERE BE A PHYSICAL RELEASE?

Not of the demo. But I would love to make a physical release of the final game, but it requires a certain amount of financial success to be viable.


WHERE CAN I FIND THE SOUNDTRACK?

The soundtrack will be included with the final product, along being available at my Youtube as possibly other platforms. So no soundtrack until then.


WHEN IS THE EMBARGO?

The embargo is the same as the launch date/time, that way players can try the game themselves when they discover it.
Please respect this for the sake of the players as well as other people covering the game.


HOW DO YOU PRONOUNCE 'SWALK'?

Simply the same as the word; 'walk' - but with a leading 's'.


SHOULDN'T 'SWALK' BE CAPITALIZED?

No, because I don't write my nickname that way. So it is written non-capitalized.


WHAT GAMES INSPIRED YOU?

I have far too many inspirations to list them all, but to name a few:
1976 Adventure, Castlevania series, Zelda II and the Dark Souls series.


WILL THE GAME BE TRANSLATED TO X AND Y LANGUAGE?

I would love to have the game translated to as many languages as possible.
However, with no budget behind the game, I don't have the option to hire translators and will have to rely on community effort.
For such efforts, I distribute the game with the Localization.csv file, so anyone can freely add a language and send it to me.
